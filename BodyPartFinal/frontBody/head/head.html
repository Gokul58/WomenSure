Head


A head is the part of an organism which usually includes the ears, brain, forehead, cheeks, chin, eyes, nose, and mouth, each of which aid in various sensory functions such as sight, hearing, smell, and taste, respectively. Some very simple animals may not have a head, but many bilaterally symmetric forms do, regardless of size.

Featured Yoga

Reclined Bound Angle

  1. Lie on your back on the yoga mat with your legs extended and hands resting by your side.
  2. Bend the knees of both the legs and bring the soles of both the legs together at the centre.
  3. The edges of the feet should be resting on the ground.
  4. Stretch your hand outward breathe in and out slowly.
  5. Stay in this position for a few seconds and try to relax.
  6. Come back to the starting position and repeat the same 5-10 times.

Cold therapy

  1. Wrap an ice pack in a towel and apply it to your forehead (10 minutes on, 10 minutes off). Cold therapy can reduce inflammation and dull the sensation of pain.
